Learning HTML: Understanding the Paragraph Tag (<p>)

Introduction

Welcome to the lesson on the <p> tag! The paragraph tag is one of the most commonly used tags in HTML, allowing you to create blocks of text in your webpage.

Explanation

The <p> tag defines a paragraph of text. When you place your text between <p> and </p>, the text will be displayed as a block on your webpage, separated from other elements.

Example

Letâ€™s see an example in code:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
    <title>My First Paragraph</title>
</head>
<body>
    <p>This is my first paragraph!</p>
</body>
</html>

When you run this code, you'll see "This is my first paragraph!" appear as a block of text on the page.
